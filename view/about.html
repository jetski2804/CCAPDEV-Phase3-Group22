Features

Write Reviews

Submit star ratings and detailed reviews for any dining spot near DLSU.

Secure Authentication

Register and log in with bcrypt-hashed passwords and server-side session management.

Helpful Votes

Upvote or downvote reviews to surface the most useful student feedback.

Search & Browse

Search spots by name, cuisine type, or keyword with real-time suggestions.

Sort Reviews

Sort by newest, highest rated, or most helpful to find what you need.

User Profiles

View your review history and update your display name and bio anytime.

Architecture

CampusTaste follows the Model–View–Controller (MVC) pattern. Models are defined as Mongoose schemas (User, Post, Reply) in model/models.js. Views are the HTML files (index.html, spot.html, profile.html, etc.) served statically by Express. Controllers are the Express route handlers in controller/controllers.js that process requests, apply validation, interact with the database, and return responses to the views. Front-end interactivity and data fetching are handled by scripts.js via the Fetch API.
